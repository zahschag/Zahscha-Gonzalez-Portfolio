About me

Hi, my name is Zahscha (Sa-sha), born in VA in 1989, lived in Puerto Rico most of my life. After finishing my associates degree in digital graphic design from Atlantic University College in Guaynabo PR, and completed an internship at the Puerto Rico Art Museum in Santurce Puerto Rico. I ventured my way into one of the tech motropolis of the United States, MD. Attending online at Full Sail University I graduated with a Web Design & Developer bachelor of science degree. I love the simple things, art, nature... and of course the beach. I am a fine believer that inspiration can be found in the most simple ways and places ever. We just have to look hard enough.
